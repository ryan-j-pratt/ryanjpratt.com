About Me

I’m a Boston-based data analyst working at the intersection of public policy, economics, and data. I currently serve as a Budget and Legislative Analyst for the Boston City Council, where I support the City’s annual budget process and help translate complex data into actionable insights for decision-makers. My work focuses on making messy, disparate data useful.
